Understanding Quality Parameters

Learn what each quality metric means and how it affects your milk pricing

Fat Content

Measures the richness of milk. Higher fat content (3.5%+) commands premium pricing.

Target: 3.5-4.5%

SNF Content

Includes proteins, lactose, and minerals. Higher SNF indicates better nutritional value.

Target: 8.5%+

Protein Level

Essential for nutrition and cheese-making. Higher protein content increases market value.

Target: 3.2%+

Bacterial Count

Indicates milk safety and hygiene. Lower bacterial count ensures longer shelf life.

Target: <100K CFU/ml
